About the Quiz

The TIU WB ACM Students Chapter invites all students to join a thrilling quiz competition testing Computer Science, GK and reasoning.

Quiz Format

Eligibility & Criteria

  • Teams of 3 members can register.
  • Each team will have a unique team ID.

Format

Level 1: Selection Round

  • Duration: 10 minutes
  • Question Format: 10 questions (5 General Mathematics, 5 Aptitude/Logical Reasoning/GK)
  • Selection: Top 10 teams out of 20 will qualify for the next level.

Level 2

Round 1: Logical and Theoretical Round

  • Question Format: Core subjects (Hardware, Software, Trends, History of Computing)
  • Question Levels: Intermediate and Advanced
  • Question Types: Theoretical questions

Level 2

Round 2: Technical Round

  • Question Format: Error Finding, Array Sorting, Code Completion, Debugging etc.
  • Question Levels: Intermediate and Advanced

Marking Scheme

  • Each question: 10 marks
  • Correct answer: +10 marks
  • Incorrect answer: -5 marks
  • Challenge: +20 marks (if correct), -10 marks (if incorrect)
  • Maximum 2 challenges per question

General

  • Carry ID cards
  • No mobiles during quiz

Rules & Regulations

  • Teams must adhere to the time limits for each round.
  • No extra time will be given under any circumstances.
  • Teams are not allowed to use any digital devices (Androids, laptops, tablets, etc.) during the competition.
  • All digital devices must be switched off and kept in bags, which will be placed in a designated area under supervision.
  • Teams must respect the decisions of the judges.
  • Challenges can only be made maximum twice per question.
  • Challenged answer must be different from the previous answer.
  • Same team can not challenge more than once per question.
